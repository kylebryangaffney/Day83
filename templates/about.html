About Me

This is what I do.

Thank you for visiting my website.

My audio journey began in middle school when I decided to splice together two takes for an audition recording, a move that went potentially unnoticed by my incredibly patient band director.

Following this spark of curiosity, I pursued my passion at Berklee College of Music, where I delved into music production and engineering while evolving my saxophone playing.

Upon relocating to Los Angeles, I dove face first into music studios. And after I cleaned enough toilets, I was able to hone my ears and skills and eventually collaborate with industry professionals.

While making records is absolutely rad, it proved unsustainable for me. This journey eventually led me to accept a sponsored cross-country adventure, where a friend and I embarked on an 88-day journey from the Santa Monica Pier to Times Square, traversing by foot, rollerblades, and bicycles.

Returning to reality, I transitioned into working on TV and film projects, collaborating with top-tier artists across animation, live-action television, theatrical releases, and video games. These diverse experiences have shaped me into an analytical and performance-driven professional.

In 2023, I embarked on a new journey into software development, leveraging my background in audio engineering to explore the dynamic intersection of technology and audio production.
